My Early Life

I was born on June 5th, 2001 at the Naval Hospital in Bremerton, WA. I was kind of an awkward kid growing up. I didn't have a ton of friends but I always tried to get along with others. My very first computer was an old laptop running Windows XP.

Work Experience

I got my first job in Late Spring 2024 as a government contractor for the Puget Sound Naval Shipyard as a provision loader. I load food onto naval vessels. It can be a tough job, but I enjoy doing it. I also started up an indie video game studio with a fellow alumni from my last school, we don't have any projects yet, but we're hoping to have something within the next few years!

Game Development Experience

I originally went to the Academy of Interactive Entertainment in Seattle, WA from 2020-2022 to study Game Design and Production, it is there where I proceeded to get my experience developing video games. As a result of going there and working in my free time, I have gained experience working in the game engines Godot, Unity, and Unreal Engine. Game Development is my life-long passion alongside being a programmer and I really hope at some point in my life that it's what I can do as a living.
